1. Abstract

Traction algebra is the result of applying Constructive Operational Type Theory to ordinary algebra. By demanding totality and reversibility in every operation, a rich structure emerges in base-0 exponentiation and logarithm. This article discusses one of the most striking emergent structures as a result of following this methodology: the zero-lift Chebyshev polynomials.
