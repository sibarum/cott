1. What is it?

RWKV is my favorite conversational AI, despite having a name that's impossible to pronounce. Very few people know about this language model, and while it's certainly not the smartest model available, its unique technical design enables it to be used in creative ways that would not be possible with any mainstream models. I wanted to share these unique capabilities, but they're a bit hard to explain, so I've decided to make a demo of it.

You could just scroll down to the demo if you want to see it. Otherwise, let me tell you what's so unique about RWKV.

All language models have a hidden state, frequently called the "context". The size of a model's context directly affects how much conversation it can remember, but it's a double-edged sword; too large and it will take too much memory to run. Most modern LLMs are too large to run on a normal PC, and many require expensive aftermarket hardware.
