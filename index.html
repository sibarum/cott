About BeigePapers:

"BeigePapers" is a play on words, meaning "not quite whitepapers," in the sense that it hasn't been peer-reviewed or published through any official channels, unless otherwise noted.

Everything here is a work-in-progress. The goal is to document everything from "barely an idea" to "full-fledged theory" and everything in-between. This format is intended to be more casual, speculative, and experimental. You may find typos, grammatical errors, or inconsistencies. If you do, you're welcome to contribute! These pages are hosted on a Github repository.

Content Portability

Whitepapers are typically intended for print media, which are quite limited in their capabilities. Printed copies don't support interactivity, and often have difficulties in copy+pasting text even if exported to a portable PDF format with expensive software.

BeigePapers may contain interactive demonstrations, math equations can be copied and pasted to anywhere, and yet the page can still be printed without losing content or wasting precious ink.
